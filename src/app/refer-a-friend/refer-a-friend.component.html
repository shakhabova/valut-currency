Refer A Friend

Rewards For You & Your Friends

When your friend signs-up using your referral link and transfers £50, we add £5 to your account balance to be used towards your next transfer.

1. Share a referral link

Go to the Refer a Friend page, login, and then invite friends by sharing your personal referral link.

2. Invite friends to send money

Your friends will get the benefit of sending money to their loved ones and can invite further friends.

3. Get rewarded

You will get rewarded £5 for each referral after your friends register themselves and make minimum transfer.
